Year 2014

Year 2014 was the year of inception (Not the 2010 Nolan's Inception flick) for <web/F>. Only event we conducted in 2014 was Front-end architecture. We were trying to figure out bits and pieces of developer conference management. Right from arranging water to selection of venue, everything was new. We noted every possible feedback from our attendees and then laid track for the future of <web/F>. It only makes sense that we dedicate an entire blog post for this.
